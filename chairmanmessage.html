Chairman,
Abbas Institute of Medical Sciences

Chairman's Message

A man’s true wealth is the good he does in the world.” Every human being on this green planet has a right to love, and be loved and grow up in an atmosphere of love, affection and of moral and material security. This is possible only when cooperative efforts are made to make it. Charities exist to create a better society. The range and scope of their work and the variety of people they help is really encouraging.

The focus of Abbas Institute of Medical Sciences is not just limited to particular section but to all those who are needy. I have always held the belief that through cooperation, innovation and grass root efforts our young people can transform this country. Whether rich or poor, the next generation has the power to change this world for the better.
